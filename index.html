SCARA Game

This is a mildly tongue-in-cheek gamification of the interface and technical advancements proposed and or presented by the arm project.

The end result will have stylized virtual demonstrations of key technologies such as

  • Photopolymer printing
  • Laser cutting
  • Inspection
  • Machining
